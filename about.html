About SeekEasy

Use Seekeasy to find Happy Hours based on day of the week, in the Baker neighborhood.

Just select a day, and SeekEasy present you with a list of restaurants that have happy hours on that day, and the hours for the happy hour.

Use the Add button on the homepage to add restaurants to our dataset. Added restaruants will appear in search results.

SeekEasy is designed for mobile use.
